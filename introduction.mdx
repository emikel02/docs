## What Smelt Does

- **Reads your lead data** — Job titles, companies, industries, locations, and any other CSV columns
- **Generates unique copy** — AI creates personalized hooks, pain points, and CTAs for each lead
- **Scales infinitely** — Process 10,000 leads and get 10,000 unique outputs
- **Saves you money** — BYOK pricing is 10-50x cheaper than per-credit tools
